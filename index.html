Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 20, 2025
Your Privacy is Our Priority

SpendWise is a completely offline expense tracker app. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to external servers. All your financial information stays on your device.

1. Introduction

Welcome to SpendWise! This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our expense tracking application. SpendWise is designed with privacy as a core principle - we believe your financial data belongs to you and should remain under your control.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

SpendWise operates entirely offline on your device. We do not collect, store, access, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Financial data (bank accounts, transactions, expenses, income)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or statistics
  • Device information
  • Any other personal or sensitive information

3. How Your Data is Stored

All data you create in SpendWise is stored locally on your device. This includes:

  • Categories: Custom expense and income categories you create
  • Accounts: Your financial accounts information
  • Budgets: Budget limits and tracking data
  • Records: All transaction records (expenses and income)
  • Analysis Data: Calculated statistics and visualizations

This data is stored exclusively in your device's local storage and is never transmitted to any external servers or third parties.

4. Backup and Export Features

SpendWise provides backup and export functionality to help you secure your data:

Backup Feature

When you create a backup, all your data is exported to a file that is saved on your device. You have complete control over where this backup file is stored and how it is shared. The backup file remains on your device unless you choose to transfer it elsewhere.

Export Feature

The export feature allows you to extract your financial records in standard formats (such as CSV or JSON) for use in other applications or for your own record-keeping. Exported files are saved locally to your device, and you decide what to do with them.

Important: If you choose to store backup or exported files in cloud storage services (Google Drive, Dropbox, etc.) or share them with others, those services' privacy policies will apply. We recommend encrypting sensitive financial data before storing it in any cloud service.

5. Data Security

Since SpendWise operates completely offline:

  • Your data is not exposed to internet-based security threats
  • No data breaches can occur from our servers (because we don't have any)
  • Your device's security features protect your data

We recommend that you:

  • Keep your device secured with a password, PIN, or biometric lock
  • Regularly create backups of your data
  • Store backup files in secure locations
  • Be cautious when sharing exported data

6. Third-Party Services

SpendWise does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or external APIs. The app functions entirely independently on your device without any external connections.

7. Children's Privacy

SpendWise does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since the app operates completely offline and stores no data externally, it can be safely used by individuals of all ages.

8. Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data in SpendWise:

  • Access: All your data is accessible within the app at any time
  • Modification: You can edit or delete any records, categories, accounts, or budgets
  • Deletion: You can delete all data by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app
  • Export: You can export your data at any time for backup or migration purposes

9. Data Retention

Your data remains on your device for as long as you choose to keep it. Data is only removed when you:

  • Manually delete records or categories within the app
  • Clear the app's data through your device settings
  • Uninstall the application

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app's functionality or legal requirements. When we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

11. Open Source and Transparency

SpendWise is committed to transparency. Users can verify our privacy claims by reviewing the app's source code if it is made publicly available. Our offline-first architecture ensures that your data privacy is built into the core design of the application.
