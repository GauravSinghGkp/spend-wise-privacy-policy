Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 15, 2025
Your Privacy is Our Priority

SpendWise is an offline expense tracker app that keeps your financial data on your device. However, we use Google AdMob to display advertisements, which may collect certain information for ad personalization and analytics. Please read this policy carefully to understand how your information is handled.

1. Introduction

Welcome to SpendWise! This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our expense tracking application. SpendWise is designed with privacy as a core principle - your financial data stays on your device and is never transmitted to our servers. However, we do use third-party advertising services that may collect certain information as described below.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

SpendWise itself does not collect, store, access, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Financial data (bank accounts, transactions, expenses, income)
  • Usage analytics or statistics about your financial activities
  • Any other personal or sensitive financial information

3. Information Collected by Third-Party Services (Google AdMob)

Important: Advertising Data Collection

SpendWise uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect certain information to provide personalized ads and improve advertising services.

Google AdMob Data Collection

Google AdMob may collect and process the following types of information:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)
  • Ad Interaction Data: Information about your interactions with ads (views, clicks, conversions)
  • Location Information: Approximate location based on IP address (not precise GPS location)
  • Usage Data: App usage statistics, crash reports, and performance data
  • Cookie and Similar Technologies: Advertising identifiers and related technologies

Purpose of AdMob Data Collection

The information collected by Google AdMob is used to:

  • Display relevant advertisements based on your interests
  • Measure ad performance and effectiveness
  • Prevent fraud and improve ad quality
  • Provide analytics to advertisers (in aggregated form)

Your Advertising Choices

You can control personalized advertising through:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization or Reset Advertising ID
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → Turn off Personalized Ads or Limit Ad Tracking
  • Google Ad Settings: Visit adssettings.google.com to manage your ad preferences

4. How Your Financial Data is Stored

All financial data you create in SpendWise is stored locally on your device and is never shared with us or any third parties, including AdMob. This includes:

  • Categories: Custom expense and income categories you create
  • Accounts: Your financial accounts information
  • Budgets: Budget limits and tracking data
  • Records: All transaction records (expenses and income)
  • Analysis Data: Calculated statistics and visualizations

This data is stored exclusively in your device's local storage and is never transmitted to any external servers or third parties.

5. Backup and Export Features

SpendWise provides backup and export functionality to help you secure your data:

Backup Feature

When you create a backup, all your data is exported to a file that is saved on your device. You have complete control over where this backup file is stored and how it is shared. The backup file remains on your device unless you choose to transfer it elsewhere.

Export Feature

The export feature allows you to extract your financial records in standard formats (such as CSV or JSON) for use in other applications or for your own record-keeping. Exported files are saved locally to your device, and you decide what to do with them.

Important: If you choose to store backup or exported files in cloud storage services (Google Drive, Dropbox, etc.) or share them with others, those services' privacy policies will apply. We recommend encrypting sensitive financial data before storing it in any cloud service.

6. Data Security

Your financial data in SpendWise is protected because it operates completely offline:

  • Your financial data is not exposed to internet-based security threats
  • No data breaches of your financial information can occur from our servers (because we don't have any)
  • Your device's security features protect your data

We recommend that you:

  • Keep your device secured with a password, PIN, or biometric lock
  • Regularly create backups of your data
  • Store backup files in secure locations
  • Be cautious when sharing exported data

Advertisements displayed in SpendWise may contain links to third-party websites or apps. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

8. Children's Privacy

SpendWise does not knowingly collect any personal financial information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. However, Google AdMob may collect device and usage information as described in their privacy policy. We recommend that parents or guardians supervise children's use of the app and manage advertising settings on their devices.

9. Your Rights and Control

You have control over your data in SpendWise:

  • Financial Data: You have complete control - access, modify, or delete any records, categories, accounts, or budgets at any time
  • Advertising Data: You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings (see section 3 above)
  • Data Deletion: You can delete all app data by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app
  • Export: You can export your financial data at any time for backup or migration purposes

10. Data Retention

Your financial data remains on your device for as long as you choose to keep it. Data is only removed when you:

  • Manually delete records or categories within the app
  • Clear the app's data through your device settings
  • Uninstall the application

For advertising data retained by Google AdMob, please refer to Google's data retention policies.
